Dark Comedy? Absurdist Drama? Naked Confessional? Laughing Wild is nearly impossible to categorize. Through its raw and vulnerable confessional format, and drop-of-a-dime switches from high to low, Laughing Wild is a fascinating journey through the minds of two complex individuals trying their best to overcome adversity and laugh wild amid severest woe. It's just too difficult to be alive, isn't it?
